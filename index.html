Strategy

Three Bets.
One Destination.

We don't need five initiatives. We need three bold moves
executed with discipline.

Bet 1

Own the Bake-Off Channel

  • Build a C-store offensive. Gas stations and convenience chains are adding bake-off ovens across Europe. Aryzta owns this channel. We don't. That must change.
  • Create turnkey packages. Oven + product + planogram + training. Make it effortless for non-bakery retailers to launch bake-off.
  • Go digital. B2B ordering platform for smaller customers. Automated restock. Lower our cost-to-serve, increase lock-in.
Why Now

The channel is being claimed right now

Aryzta and Vandemoortele are signing multi-year supply agreements with major gas station and convenience chains. Every deal we miss is a customer locked out for 3-5 years.

Bet 2

Build the Savoury Platform

  • Boboli is the beachhead. EUR 50M in revenue, state-of-the-art bakery in Netherlands, strong positions in Benelux, UK, Germany. Now scale it across all 60+ markets.
  • Panificio adds Italian authenticity. Par-baked ciabatta, baguette, focaccia — the fastest-growing bake-off subcategories in European retail.
  • Pizza crusts: 6.3% CAGR. The single fastest-growing product category. We now have the production base. Time to claim market share.
Revenue Potential

EUR 200-300M additional by 2030

Cross-selling Boboli and Panificio products through our existing customer relationships in 60+ markets. The distribution is already built.

Bet 3

Monetize Our Sustainability Story

  • Stop hiding our best asset. We're owned by 17,000 farmers with a Climate & Nature program targeting carbon-neutral farming by 2050. No competitor has anything close.
  • Win the retail tenders. EU packaging regulation (2025/40) makes sustainability mandatory. We should be the supplier retailers call first — not an afterthought.
  • Launch a consumer brand around it. "Climate & Nature" should be on packaging, not buried in a CSR report. Consumers pay 15-20% more for credible sustainability claims.
Moat

The one thing money can't buy

Aryzta can buy factories. Europastry can buy brands. Vandemoortele can buy Delifrance. But none of them can buy 17,000 farmers and 100+ years of cooperative heritage. This is our moat — if we use it.

Decisions

Three Decisions for the CEO

1

Commit EUR 30M to a dedicated C-store bake-off unit

Hire a team, build the turnkey model, sign 3 pilot chains by Q4 2026. If we wait, the channel will be locked by competitors for years.

2

Fast-track Boboli and Panificio integration into all markets

Don't let these acquisitions sit as standalone operations. Within 12 months, every Unibake sales team should be selling savoury. Goal: 15+ markets by end of 2027.

3

Put "Climate & Nature" on the product, not just the website

Rebrand Schulstad and Hatting packaging with visible sustainability credentials. Make it the default reason retailers choose us over Vandemoortele or Aryzta.
