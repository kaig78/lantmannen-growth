Bake-Off Channels

Where Bake-Off Is Growing Fastest

High Growth

Gas Stations & C-Stores

The convenience store segment is expanding at the highest CAGR. Urban consumers want fresh bakery on-the-go. Gas station chains across Europe are adding bake-off ovens — converting from packaged snacks to freshly baked products with higher margins.

Core Channel

In-Store Bakeries (Retail)

Supermarkets are systematically replacing scratch bakeries with bake-off. Lower labor cost, zero waste from unsold dough, consistent quality. Lidl, Aldi, Carrefour are all scaling bake-off programs aggressively.

Expanding

HoReCa & QSR

Hotels, restaurants, cafes, and quick-service chains need consistent, premium-looking bakery without a baker on staff. Bake-off viennoiserie and artisan bread are the sweet spot.

Emerging

E-Commerce & Dark Kitchens

Online grocery platforms and delivery kitchens are new outlets. Frozen bake-off products ship and store perfectly — ideal for the last-mile bakery experience at home.

Key Takeaways

What We Can Learn from Competitors

Threat

Vandemoortele + Delifrance

This mega-merger creates a European bake-off powerhouse. Combined, they will have massive scale in frozen viennoiserie and private label bake-off — directly competing with Unibake's core business.

Lesson

Europastry's Premium Play

Europastry proves that premium positioning works in bake-off. Their IPO success (EUR 210M for growth) shows investor confidence in high-end frozen bakery. Unibake should double down on Pastridor.

Lesson

Aryzta's C-Store Focus

Aryzta has built strong partnerships with convenience and QSR chains. This is a channel Unibake should aggressively enter — gas stations and convenience stores are underpenetrated for bake-off.

Unibake Advantage

Farmer-Owned Sustainability Story

No competitor can match Lantmannen's field-to-shelf authenticity. As retailers demand sustainable suppliers, this is an increasingly powerful differentiator in bake-off tenders.

Lever 2

Win the Bake-Off Race

  • C-Store offensive: Build dedicated bake-off programs for gas station and convenience chains (like Aryzta does)
  • Portion innovation: Mini-formats, snack bites, grab-and-go solutions for on-the-move consumers
  • Turnkey solutions: Oven + product + planogram packages for non-bakery retailers entering bake-off
  • Digital ordering: Build a B2B platform for smaller customers to order, track, and restock automatically
  • Premium bake-off: Leverage Pastridor for high-end viennoiserie in hotels and specialty cafes
Market Shift

Bake-off becomes #1 supply method in 2026

This is a once-in-a-generation structural shift. Every retailer, gas station, and food outlet is a potential bake-off customer. Unibake's frozen expertise is perfectly positioned — but speed matters.

Lever 3

  • Develop protein-enriched breads, rolls, and snacks
  • High-fiber formulations for institutional clients (schools, hospitals)
  • Launch plant-based product lines under existing brands
  • Clean-label positioning: less sugar, salt, natural ingredients
